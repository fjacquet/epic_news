# Use Cases — Business & Intelligence

Crews that help with sales, meetings, OSINT investigations, and legal review.

[← Back to User Guide](../user_guide.md)

## 💼 Sales Prospecting (SalesProspectingCrew)

**What it does:** Finds and researches sales contacts and decision-makers at target companies.

**Sample prompts:**

- "Find sales contacts at OpenAI in France."
- "Identify decision-makers at Stripe Switzerland."

**Output:** List of contacts + research notes (HTML).

## 🤝 Meeting Prep (MeetingPrepCrew)

**What it does:** Prepares you for meetings with company research, participant profiles, and strategic talking points.

**Sample prompts:**

- "Prepare for a meeting with Microsoft."
- "Prepare for a meeting with the marketing team to discuss Q2 strategy."
- "Research Google before our call."

**Output:** Meeting prep brief (HTML) with agenda, participants, talking points.

## 🕵️ OSINT Intelligence (OsintCrew & CompanyProfilerCrew)

**What it does:** Conducts deep open-source intelligence: company profile, tech stack, web presence, HR analytics, geospatial footprint.

**Sample prompts:**

- "Full intelligence report on OpenAI."
- "Complete OSINT analysis of Mistral.AI."
- "Investigate recent cybersecurity incidents affecting European banks."

**Output:** Structured multi-section HTML report.

## ⚖️ Legal Analysis (LegalAnalysisCrew)

**What it does:** Analyzes legal documents, contracts, or cases for risks and compliance issues.

**Sample prompts:**

- "Review this contract for potential risks."
- "Analyse ce contrat pour les risques."

**Output:** Legal analysis report (HTML).
