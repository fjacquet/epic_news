# Use Cases — Lifestyle & Creative

Crews that help with cooking, travel, daily inspirations, and creative writing.

[← Back to User Guide](../user_guide.md)

## 🍳 Cooking (CookingCrew)

**What it does:** Generates a recipe (Thermomix-optimized when relevant) in HTML and Paprika-3-compatible YAML, ready to import into the Paprika app.

**Sample prompts:**

- "Plan a vegetarian dinner for four."
- "Generate a French ratatouille recipe for Paprika app."
- "Recipe for chocolate chip cookies."
- "How to make beef bourguignon?"

**Output:** HTML recipe + Paprika YAML (sent as email attachments).

## 🍽️ Menu Planning (MenuDesignerCrew)

**What it does:** Builds a complete weekly menu planner with ~30 recipes and an organized shopping list.

**Sample prompts:**

- "Generate a complete weekly menu planner with 30 recipes and shopping list for a family of 3 in French."

**Output:** HTML weekly grid + shopping list.

## ✈️ Travel Planning (HolidayPlannerCrew)

**What it does:** Creates detailed travel itineraries with activities, restaurants, transport, and local insights.

**Sample prompts:**

- "Plan a 5-day trip to Tokyo."
- "Plan a 7-day trip to Japan with cultural and food experiences."
- "Holiday itinerary for Paris in spring."
- "Weekend getaway to Barcelona."

**Output:** HTML itinerary with day-by-day breakdown.

## ⛪ Saint of the Day (SaintDailyCrew)

**What it does:** Returns the Catholic saint of the day with biography, feast details, and Swiss connection if any.

**Sample prompts:**

- "Who is the saint of the day?"
- "Donne-moi le saint du jour en français."

**Output:** HTML saint profile.

## ✍️ Creative Writing (PoemCrew)

**What it does:** Generates creative poems on any topic in any style (haiku, sonnet, free verse).

**Sample prompts:**

- "Write a poem about spring in Paris."
- "Write a poem about artificial intelligence."
- "Poem about the ocean at sunset."

**Output:** HTML poem with stanzas + analysis.
