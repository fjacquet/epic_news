# Use Cases — Finance

This page lists the finance-oriented crews and the prompts that trigger them.

[← Back to User Guide](../user_guide.md)

## 📈 Finance (FinDailyCrew)

**What it does:** Analyzes your stock and crypto portfolio (sourced from `data/stock.csv` and the Kraken API) and produces a professional HTML report (in French) with BUY / SELL / KEEP recommendations for each asset.

**Sample prompts:**

- "Give me a daily financial summary of my stocks and crypto."
- "Analyse mon portefeuille boursier et crypto et donne-moi des recommandations."
- "Analyze my portfolio and suggest trades."
- "Give me today's financial analysis."
- "What should I buy or sell today?"

**Output:** HTML report in French, sent by email if Gmail is connected via Composio.

## 🛒 Shopping Advisor (ShoppingAdvisorCrew)

**What it does:** Researches a product, compares prices in Switzerland and France, runs competitor analysis, and produces a buy-recommendation card.

**Sample prompts:**

- "I need shopping advice for a MacBook Pro M4."
- "Compare prices for an iPhone 15 Pro in Switzerland and France."
- "Best laptop under 1500 CHF with pros and cons."

**Output:** Structured HTML report with side-by-side price tables and pros/cons.
